HP Pavilion x360 Laptop - 14t-dw100

Manual (Maintenance and Service Guide)

Hardware: 128GB M.2 Drive (model rbu-sns8350des3128gp)
Upgrade / Replacement: Best Laptop 1TB M2 Drive

Problem: When using the laptop on it's side HP's software + hardware automatically disable the keyboard and touchpad, even when auto-screen rotation and tablet mode are both disabled.
Solution:

  • Open "Device Manager" (windows key then type "device manager")
  • Click "System devices" do expand the dropdown
  • Right click "Intel(R) Integrated Sensor Solution" and click "Disable device"
    • To re-enable the device follow these same steps but hit "Enable device" instead of disable.
